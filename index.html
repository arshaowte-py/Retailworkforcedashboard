How it works
1
Staff fill the Google Form each evening — selecting their store, entering their name, role, and availability for tomorrow.
2
The form response lands in the linked Google Sheet automatically.
3
Dashboard reads the Sheet CSV every 5 minutes. Whoever fills the form, at whatever store they select, appears on the dashboard — floaters, new joiners, everyone.
4
Names are fuzzy-matched so typos like "Krushna" / "Krishna" are handled automatically.
Sheet → File → Share → Publish to web → Responses tab → CSV → Publish → copy URL. Must contain pub and output=csv.
